# Ethics and Misuse Boundaries

Tracewright is a tool for careful reading, not exposure.

## Core Rule

Do not use this tool to accuse, shame, expose, or rank people based on suspected AI use.

## Intended Uses

- slowing down premature judgment
- documenting uncertainty
- separating evidence from intuition
- preserving alternative explanations
- supporting provenance-aware editorial review
- training reviewers to inspect AI-mediated text carefully

## Non-Uses

- identifying whether a person used AI
- policing private correspondence
- generating accusations
- producing authenticity scores
- replacing consent or human review

## Privacy

Public demos should use synthetic, anonymized, or consent-safe materials only. Private letters, names, identifiable details, and raw correspondence should not be published without explicit consent.

## Human Judgment

A reviewer should always distinguish:

- what the text says
- what is known about how the text was made
- what is inferred from style or context
- what remains unknown

The purpose is not to decide faster. The purpose is to decide more carefully.
