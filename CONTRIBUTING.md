# Contributing

Tracewright welcomes careful improvements that preserve the project's core purpose: slowing down premature judgment.

## Principles

- Do not turn Tracewright into an AI detector.
- Do not add authenticity scores, human-likeness scores, or AI-likeness scores.
- Do not add classifier-style language or pass/fail labels.
- Do not add real private materials to demos.
- Use synthetic, public, anonymized, or consent-safe examples.
- Preserve alternative explanations.
- Keep source roles visible.
- Keep reader intuition separate from evidence.
- Respect the non-commercial license and commercial-use restrictions.

## Methodology Changes

Open an issue or proposal before changing core review lanes, posture labels, evidence-card structure, or misuse boundaries.

## Demo Data

Demo data should show safe review behavior. It should not encourage accusation, exposure, ranking, or private-material upload.
